Quick Sort

Quicksort is the quickest and one of the most efficient sorting algorithm. Similar to Merge sort, Quick sort follows Divide and conquer algorithm to sort the given array. The quicksort algorithm is a sorting algorithm that sorts an array by choosing a pivot element, and partition the array around the pivot such that Elements smaller than the pivot are moved to the left of pivot, and elements larger than the pivot are moved to the right of pivot. It continues to choose a pivot element and break down the array into single-element array, before combing them back together to form a single sorted array.
